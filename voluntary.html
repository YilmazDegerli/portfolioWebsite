I was a very successful student throughout my school life. For this reason, I gave free lessons to both my classmates and the younger ones. Later, after I started university, I continued to give free mathematics lessons. On the other hand, I always tried to help those older than me when they needed it.


As soon as I moved to Bern, I did some research to see how I can help someone here as well. I examined aid organizations and learned that an association called Nachbarschaft Bern which introduces volunteers like me to those in need and contacted them. I liked their system so much and my wife joined us, and we've been talking to a Swiss couple for about 14 months. Now I am a member of Nachbarschaft-Bern Association. I and my wife help Swiss couple by gardening, and they help us to improve our German-speaking skills thanks to this organization. We make several activities together such as hiking, bike tour and going to the opera and theatre. We feel like a family.


I also talked to many other people about these organizations and encouraged others to join. I want to join another aid organization besides Nachbarschaft Bern after Powercoders Bootcamp. I researched and decided on help blind people. There are some associations like SZBLIND (Swiss National Association of and for the Blind) and Swiss Association of the Blind.


I believe that the greatest source of happiness for people is the good they do to other people, animals, and nature. There is nothing more comforting than this.
